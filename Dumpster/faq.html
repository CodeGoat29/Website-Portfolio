Common Questions

We keep pricing and scheduling simple. Here are the most common questions we get from homeowners, contractors, and property managers.

What can I put in the dumpster?
Household junk, renovation debris, wood, drywall, roofing shingles, yard waste, and general construction debris are all acceptable. If you are unsure about an item, call us before loading.
What items are not allowed?
Hazardous materials, chemicals, paint, oils, batteries, tires, and electronics typically require special disposal. Call us and we will guide you on what to do with restricted items.
How do I choose the right dumpster size?
Small cleanouts and minor remodels are usually a 10 or 15 yard dumpster. Larger remodels, roofing, and construction jobs often need a 20 yard or bigger. If you describe your project, we will recommend the best size.
How long can I keep the dumpster?
A few days to a few weeks is common. We do not charge per day—just tell us your timeline and we will work around your schedule.
Do you offer quick swaps or pickups?
Yes. If your dumpster fills up, we can swap it for an empty one quickly to keep your project moving.
Can you place a dumpster in my driveway?
Most driveways work fine. We place boards when needed to protect surfaces and keep things clean.
Do you handle concrete or heavy debris?
Yes. We can handle concrete removal and other heavy materials. Let us know so we can make sure you have the right size and weight limit.
How do I schedule a delivery?
Call us and we will set a delivery time that works for your project. We are a local company and can usually schedule fast.
